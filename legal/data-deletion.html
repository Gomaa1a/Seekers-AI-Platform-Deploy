Data Deletion Instructions

Seekers AI ("we") lets businesses connect their Facebook Page and Instagram account to automate customer support. You can have your data deleted at any time using either method below.

Option 1 — Remove the app from Facebook (automatic)

  1. Go to Facebook → Settings & Privacy → Settings → Apps and Websites.
  2. Find Seekers AI and click Remove.
  3. Facebook notifies us via our deletion callback and we delete the data associated with your account, returning a confirmation code you can track.

Option 2 — Request deletion directly

Email Team@seekersai.org from the address associated with your account, or the Page/Instagram owner's contact, with the subject "Data Deletion Request". We will delete your data within 30 days and confirm by email.

What gets deleted

Your account record, connected Page/Instagram tokens and IDs, and stored message and comment history. Aggregated, de-identified analytics that cannot be linked to you may be retained.
