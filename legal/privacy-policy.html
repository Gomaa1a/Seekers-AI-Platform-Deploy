Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 June 2026 · Operated by Seekers AI (www.seekersai.org) ("we", "us").

Seekers AI is an omnichannel customer-support tool that lets a business connect its Facebook Page and linked Instagram Professional account so that incoming messages and comments can be received and answered with the help of an AI agent. This policy explains what data we process and how.

1. Who this applies to

Two groups: (a) our business customers who create an account and connect their Meta assets, and (b) the end users who message or comment on those businesses' Facebook Pages and Instagram accounts.

2. Data we collect

  • Account data (business customers): name, email, and the public profile returned by Facebook Login.
  • Meta connection data: Page IDs, Instagram account IDs/usernames, and Page access tokens. Access tokens are stored encrypted (AES-256-GCM).
  • Message & comment content: the text/media of Messenger and Instagram messages and comments sent to a connected account, plus the sender's page-scoped ID (PSID), used to receive and reply to that conversation.
  • Usage & analytics: engagement metrics shown in the dashboard, and technical logs needed to operate the service.

3. How we use data

  • To receive incoming messages/comments via Meta webhooks and send replies on the connected business's behalf.
  • To generate AI-assisted responses using our AI processor.
  • To display analytics to the business that owns the connected account.
  • To secure, maintain, and support the service.

We do not sell personal data or use message content for advertising.

4. Sub-processors

We share data only with infrastructure providers needed to run the service: cloud hosting (Railway), our database/cache, and our AI model provider (Anthropic / Claude) for generating responses. Each processes data only to provide their service to us.

5. Data retention

We retain connection data and conversation history for as long as the business keeps its account active. When a Page/account is disconnected or an account is deleted, the associated data is deleted or anonymized.

6. Your rights & data deletion

End users and business customers may request deletion of their data at any time:

  • Via Facebook: removing our app from your Facebook settings triggers our deletion callback automatically.
  • Manually: see our Data Deletion Instructions or email Team@seekersai.org.
